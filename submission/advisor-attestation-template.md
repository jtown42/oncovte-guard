# Advisor Attestation Letter — Template

**Student submissions to the AMIA / HL7 FHIR App Competition must include a PDF
of this letter, signed by the student's primary advisor.** Fill in every
`[BRACKETED]` field, delete this instruction block and the checklist at the
bottom, print on the training program's official letterhead, have the primary
advisor sign and date it, and export to PDF for upload.

The competition requires the letter to confirm items (a)–(d): the training
program's name and address; clear identification of the primary advisor; all
co-authors and each author's contribution; and an attestation of the student's
contribution to the development of the application.
